Pasta With Sausage

Description

Commom dish for those who are in a hurry

Ingredients

  • 50g of pasta
  • 70g of sausage
  • 1 packing of tomato sauce
  • 500ml of water
  • salt

Steps

  1. Heat the water in a pan
  2. When boiling, put the pasta and add salt
  3. In another pan, heat the tomato sauce
  4. Add the sausages in the tomato sauce and let it cook
  5. When both are ready, mix them in the plate
