Prato Feito

Description:

This is a commom brazilian dish. That's what we usually eat at lunch and dinner. It is simple to make and provides all the necessary nutricinal components that we need.

Ingredients

  • 100g rice
  • 100g beans
  • 150g beef
  • 3 pieces of garlic
  • 1 tomato
  • water
  • salt
  • 2 potatoes

Steps

Rice
  1. In a pre heated pan, add a little bit of butter
  2. Fry the garlic and onion cut in small pieces
  3. When it is smelling, add the rice and mix
  4. Add water untill it hits one finger above the rice
  5. Add salt as your preference
  6. Let it cook with the pan semi open
Beans
  1. Do exactly the same as rice, but in a pressure pan
  2. Obs: the water needs to be, at least, four finger above the beans
Beef
  1. Season it with salt as your preference
  2. Add a little bit of butter in a pre heated pan
  3. Fry the beef on both sides
Fried Potatoes
  1. Put some soy oil in a pan and let it heat
  2. Once hot, add the potatoes cut in tires and let it fry
  3. One fried, rest them on a paper towell
