Tapioca

Description

Commom brazilian food, made with the gum of a plant with the same name.

You can stuff with anything you like, it can be salty or sweet.

Besides, this food can be a meal or a simple snack

Ingredients

  • 50g of tapioca gum
  • 10g of butter
  • Stuff of your preference

Steps

  1. Sift the gum in a frying pan, filling all over bottom
  2. Heat over low heat
  3. In one minute, more or less, you can add the stuff you want to warrant that it will heat together
  4. If you do not want a stuff, you can take off when it hardens
  5. If you want a stuff, you can take off when the stuff is to your linking
  6. Watch out to not burn it
  7. Rub the butter
